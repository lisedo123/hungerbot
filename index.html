About Our Mission.

UNITED AGAINST HUNGER

My sister and I are teaming up for a competition where
we're developing a website dedicated to addressing food
insecurity. Our project focuses on providing essential
resources for people in need, featuring options as
donation portals, displaying ongoing drives, and allowing
users to organize and add their own initiatives. With this
endeavor, we aim to make a tangible difference in combating
hunger and fostering community support.
